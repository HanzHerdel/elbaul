El Baul Spanish School

Who we Are

Centro Lingüístico El Baúl is a center for learning Spanish as a second language, cultural exchange and volunteer opportunities located in the Western Guatemalan highlands. CLB was founded in 1994 by a group of Guatemalan Language professionals, active in community, social and environmental issues in Quetzaltenango, Guatemala. Off ering a comprehensive, aff ordable and people-friendly program, CLB has attracted hundreds of students from Europe, Germany, and the US. Our total immersion program ranks among the best in Quezaltenango, one of the most popular destinations for learning Spanish beyond the beaten tourist track..

OUR MISSION

• teach students to speak Spanish, from a multicultural, Indo-American perspective.
• enrich our students learning experience by introducing them to the social, historical, geographical and ecological environments of the Guatemala highlands.
• build bridges of cooperation between international students and the people of Guatemala.
• foster connections between international organizations and local community groups.
• create job opportunities and participate in economic development of local groups in Quetzaltenango, Guatemala..

OUR VISION

Instruct international people to acquire an acceptable level of communicative competence in the target language, learning Spanish cultural patterns of a community other than their own: that the student develops a cultural competence that allows a fluid communication. Compartir Guatemala and its political-social economic history..
