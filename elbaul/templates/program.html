Objectives Of Our Program

We believe that spoken communication is important to open borders between countries and overcome the obstacles of silence. In this way the social sectors and especially the popular ones can show their thought, culture, art and political social system. Promote the integral development of rural communities and excluded from the country by strengthening the community social fabric for fighting poverty, achieving better standards of living through effective and timely support for the realization of the common good. The most immediate is the work of housewife and the instructors of the Spanish language to be taxpayers for their family through the economic compensation of our students. ..
