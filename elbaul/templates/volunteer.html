Volunteer Opportunities

Centro Lingüístico El Baul maintains partnerships with many local community-based organizations. In tune with our mission statement, CLEB maintains strong community ties with local service and environ- mental organizations. As part of our Total Immer- sion Program, our students are matched with local community projects as volunteers, based on their own interests and expertise. Lending a hand to local causes is by far, one of the most memorable activities outside the classroom, for most students that respond to our past-student surveys
